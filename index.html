Turf Stable

Turf Stable prides itself on communication, honesty, and integrity.

Committed to providing you the best value in the Thoroughbred Partnership arena by charging only minimal accounting and tax preparation fees while still winning at the highest level including Grade 1, 2, and 3, Stakes winners over a period of 13 years. “ If you win, we win!” is the premise of our management structure.

There are no “management fees” that proliferate many partnerships. We receive 10% of the sale of any horse claimed or sold for $50,000 or more. The partnership will race all over the country depending where the horse fits the best. We want to run where the horse has the best opportunity to win and the most financial sense.
