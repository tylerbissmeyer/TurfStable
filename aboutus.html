History


Rusty Jones started Turf Stable in 1991 with investments in multiple Stakes Winners Lotus Pool, Weekend Madness, Star of Manila and stakes winners Mingling Glances, Harghar and Lateasusual. Turf Stable invested in 24 horses in the 90’s winning multiple Graded Stakes and a total of 12 stakes race over the 7 years of investing with the purchases earning millions of dollars in revenue with minimal investment.

In 2011 Turf Stable was rejuvenated and started creating partnerships who have won Grade 1 races, participated in 3 Breeders Cup races, running 3rd once and a filly who ran in the Kentucky Oaks. We have also won multiple Grade 2 and Grade 3 races winning a total of 18 stakes races.

Trainers will decide 99% of the time where the horses will run. If the trainer is undecided, the managing partner will contribute.

Turf Stable currently uses bloodstock agents, Doug Cauthen, Katie Cauthen, Kent Sweezey, Mike Recio, Gene Recio, and Ian Wilkes. They work as a team and agree on most of the purchases

Trainers- Ian Wilkes, Kent Sweezey, Bill Mott, Joe Sharp






Benefits


For receiving 10% on any horse sold or claimed for $50,000 or more TS provides the following:
  1. 1. TS purchases the horse and sells percentages of horses from 5% and above.
  2. 2. Manages all accounting and tax preparation. We will provide each LLC with a monthly, quarterly, or yearly accounting of all expenses and revenues. Unprecedented transparency at minimal monthly cost. An accounting firm will perform this this function at minimal costs by being involved at all stages of the billing and revenue process.
  3. 3. Total management of the horses including working with trainers, bloodstock agents, farms, accountants, suppliers and vets. TS likes to create positive relationships with everyone involved with our horses from the trainers to the hot walkers and farm hands. Outside of providing capital, the partners have no responsibilities other than HAVE FUN!
  4. 4. TS promotes the partners involvement at the races and visiting the backside. We send vet reports, articles and provide concise and timely communication as it relates to anything that could affect their investment.
  5. 5. We intend to put some Keeneland trips together for the partners in the near future and will promote partnership parties and entertaining “days at the track"
  6. 6. All partners get to vote on the names of the yearlings we purchase and TS gives everyone the opportunity to submit names that are voted on by all of the partners.
  7. 7. Primarily weekly but sometimes bi-weekly communications on all horses in training including when the horses will run. If a horse is getting time off at the farm the communication is more infrequent.
  8. 8. TS will place all owned horses after their career to be sure they have secondary careers or at a minimum the most comfortable surroundings available to retire after their racing career.
  9. 9. Each horse will be placed into its own LLC for ease of accounting and tax expenses.
  10. 10. All partners get to vote on the names of the yearlings we purchase and TS gives everyone the opportunity to submit names that are voted on by all of the partners.
  11. 11. Pictures and videos are provided on a consistent basis of the horses as they train so the partners can keep up with their physical appearance.
  12. 12. There will be help for licensing all owners.
  13. 13. Owners are always invited to attend the sales process.

There is no markup on horses if you have committed money to TS prior to any sale. If we make a purchase and someone wants to buy in within the first 2 days there will be no mark up. After that time period, risk dictates marking them up on a percentage basis with full disclosure on all purchase prices.

It is currently the intent of TS to retain 10-20% of all horses purchased.

The purchasing philosophy is you don’t have to buy expensive horses to run in stakes races. (Link to Kenwood article here.) Our goal is to find ways to compete at the highest levels through strategic purchases and strong, trustworthy contacts in the industry.
